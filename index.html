PhD Candidate @ Syracuse University

jyang142@syr.edu Syracuse, NY, USA

Hello! I am a PhD candidate at Syracuse University, advised by Prof. Kristopher Micinski Kristopher Micinski. My research focuses on program analysis and security, with an emphasis on static analysis techniques for improving the reliability and security of modern software systems.

I am also interested in security issues related to LLMs, including research on tokenizer related vulnerabilities and analysis challenges in LLM systems.

Outside of research, I enjoy exploring the outdoors and spending time on photography and snowboarding. Capturing landscapes and snowboarding in winter help me relax.
